# Privacy Policy

**TheFuel** · Last updated: June 25, 2026

TheFuel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account information:** email address used to create your account.
- **Vehicle and fuel data:** vehicle details, fuel entries, odometer readings, and car events you record in the app. This data is stored securely and synced across your devices.
- **Location data:** approximate GPS location used to detect nearby fuel stations. Location is used on-device only and is not stored on our servers.
- **Purchase information:** whether you have purchased Pro access. Payments are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store via RevenueCat — we do not store payment card details.
- **Crash logs and device identifiers:** used to diagnose errors and improve stability, processed by Sentry.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, sync, and back up your data across devices.
- To authenticate your account securely.
- To detect nearby fuel stations using your device location.
- To process and verify Pro purchases.
- To diagnose crashes and improve app stability.
- To improve the app based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate TheFuel:

- **Supabase** — database and authentication. Data is stored on servers in the EU/US. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **RevenueCat** — in-app purchase management. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- **Sentry** — crash reporting and error monitoring. [Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)
- **Google Play / Apple App Store** — app distribution and billing. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) · [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

---

## 4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

---

## 5. Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Local data on your device is encrypted. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with industry-standard security practices.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
- Export your data at any time from within the app.

---

## 7. Account and Data Deletion

Delete your account directly from within the app via **Settings → Delete account**. All associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Alternatively, email [montmobile.legal@gmail.com](mailto:montmobile.legal@gmail.com) with the subject line "Delete my account" and include the email address associated with your account.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

TheFuel is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice.
